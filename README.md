# Restaurant-Perform-Analyst
## Project Overview
The robotics industry is booming, and companies everywhere are rushing to integrate automation into their operations. But to actually win the market, understanding the data behind the sales is key. This project dives deep into robotic product sales, treating data not just as numbers, but as a roadmap to smarter business decisions. By analyzing customer behavior and product demand, this project uncovers the hidden patterns that help shape high-impact marketing strategies and drive long-term business growth.

## The Mission (Objectives)
- Check the Business Pulse: Evaluate overall sales health and tracking growth over time.
- Spot the Superstars: Identify which robotic products are the ultimate crowd-favorites and highest earners.
- Track the Money Trail: Map out exactly how much revenue comes in, broken down by quarters and years.
- Decode Buyer Behavior: Understand customer purchasing habits—knowing exactly when and why they buy.
- Empower Leadership: Build an intuitive, interactive Looker Studio dashboard that gives stakeholders real-time insights at a single glance.

## The Blueprint & Tools
Behind this project is a modern data stack built for speed and scalability:
- Google BigQuery: The heavy lifter used for storing and querying massive datasets.
- SQL: The language used to clean, aggregate, and extract meaningful metrics.
- Looker Studio: The storytelling tool used to turn raw rows of data into beautiful, interactive charts.
- GitHub: The home for version control and documentation.
